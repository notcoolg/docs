# Creating Your Account

Getting started with eChat is quick and free. All you need is an email address.

## Sign up with email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open eChat">
    Navigate to the eChat app in your browser or tap the installed app icon on your device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Sign Up">
    On the login screen, select **Sign Up** to create a new account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email and password">
    Type in your email address and choose a strong password. Your password must be at least 8 characters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from eChat and click the confirmation link. Once verified, you'll be taken into the app automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    On your first sign-in, eChat will walk you through setting up your profile, adding your name, display name, and a profile photo. See [Setting Up Your Profile](/getting-started/setting-up-your-profile) for details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sign in with EcoWestern SSO

If you already have an Economic ID, you can sign in instantly using Single Sign-On (SSO):

1. If you aren't already logged in:
2. You'll be redirected to Economic ID at `id.ecowestern.net`.
3. Sign in with your credentials.
4. You'll be returned to eChat, already logged in.

<Note>
  SSO accounts are linked to your EcoWestern identity. Your eChat profile will be associated with that account.
</Note>

Your session is remembered on the device, so you won't need to sign in again unless you explicitly log out.

## Trouble signing in?

- **Forgot your password?** Use the password reset option on the login screen. A reset link will be sent to your email.
- **Didn't get a verification email?** Check your spam/junk folder. If it's not there, try signing in again, the app will prompt you to resend the verification.
- **Account locked?** Contact EcoWestern support.
