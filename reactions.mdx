# Reactions & Replies

eChat gives you two great ways to respond to a specific message without cluttering the chat: **reactions** and **replies**.

## Reacting to a message

Reactions let you respond quickly with an emoji instead of writing a full response. They're perfect for acknowledging something, showing appreciation, or expressing a feeling at a glance.

### How to react

1. **Long-press** (mobile) or **hover and click the emoji icon** (desktop) on any message.
2. A reaction picker appears with the available emojis.
3. Tap the emoji you want to add.

### Available reactions

| Emoji | Meaning |
|---|---|
| ❤️ | Love / great |
| 👍 | Thumbs up / agree |
| 👎 | Thumbs down / disagree |
| 😂 | Laughing |
| 😮 | Surprised |
| 😢 | Sad |

### Reaction counts

Reactions are displayed beneath the message as small emoji badges with a count showing how many people reacted with that emoji. Tap a reaction badge to see who reacted.

### Removing a reaction

Tap the same emoji again on the reaction badge beneath the message to **remove your reaction**.

## Replying to a message

Replying lets you quote a specific message to give context to your response — especially useful in busy group chats.

### How to reply

1. **Long-press** (mobile) or **right-click** (desktop) on the message you want to reply to.
2. Select **Reply** from the context menu.
3. A preview of the original message appears above the input bar, showing the sender's name and message content.
4. Type your reply in the input field.
5. Tap **Send**.

### How replies look

Your reply message will display a **quoted block** at the top showing:
- The original sender's name
- A preview of their message (text or media thumbnail)

Tapping the quoted block in a reply will **scroll up** to the original message in the conversation.

### Cancelling a reply

To cancel a reply before sending:
- Tap the **✕** button next to the quoted preview above the input bar.

---

<Tip>
  In a busy group chat, always use **Reply** when responding to a specific person's message — it keeps the conversation clear and easy to follow.
</Tip>
