# Making Calls

eChat supports high-quality **audio and video calls** directly from any conversation. Calls use WebRTC for a direct, peer-to-peer connection — low-latency and high quality.

## Starting a call

You can start a call directly from a conversation:

1. Open a **DM** or **group conversation**.
2. Tap the **phone icon** (📞) for an audio call, or the **video icon** (📹) for a video call — both are in the top-right corner of the conversation header.
3. The call screen opens and the other person's device will ring.

## Receiving a call

When someone calls you:

1. An **incoming call notification** appears on your screen with the caller's name and profile photo.
2. You'll hear the **ringtone** sound (if notifications and sound are enabled).
3. Before answering, you can toggle your **microphone** and **camera** on or off using the controls in the call notification.
4. Tap **Accept** (green button) to join the call, or **Decline** (red button) to reject it.

<Note>
  If eChat is closed or in the background, an incoming call will trigger a **push notification** if you have them enabled. Tap the notification to open the app and answer.
</Note>

## During a call

Once connected, you'll see the call screen with:

| Control | Action |
|---|---|
| **Microphone icon** | Mute / unmute your mic |
| **Camera icon** | Turn your camera on / off |
| **End call button** | Leave and end the call |
| **Settings icon** | Open call settings (audio devices, echo cancellation) |

### Audio quality features

eChat's calls include several built-in audio quality improvements:
- **Echo cancellation** — Prevents your speaker audio from feeding back into your mic.
- **Noise suppression** — Filters out background noise (fans, keyboard clicks, etc.).
- **Auto gain control** — Keeps your voice at a consistent volume even if you move around.

These are enabled by default. You can toggle them in **call settings** during the call.

## Call status indicators

| Indicator | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Ringing animation | Waiting for the other person to answer |
| "Connecting…" | Establishing the peer-to-peer connection |
| Connected | Call is live |
| Reconnecting… | Temporary connection drop — eChat is trying to restore it |

## Ending a call

Tap the **red end call button** to leave the call. Both parties will hear a brief call-ended sound.

If the other person ends the call first, the call screen will close automatically.

## Call history

Your recent calls are not currently shown in a separate call log, but you can find past call conversations in your chat list — calls are initiated from and tied to a conversation.

---

<Tip>
  For the best call quality, use a wired headset or earphones with a built-in microphone, and make sure you have a stable internet connection (Wi-Fi preferred for video).
</Tip>
