## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="key" title="How do I reset my Economic ID password?">
    If you've lost access, you can trigger a secure Magic Link or password reset directly from the login portal. [View the step-by-step guide](/guides/economic-id/password-reset).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="shield-check" title="Does Azura AI train on my private data?">
    **No.** EcoWestern firmly believes in data sovereignty. Your private conversations, encrypted content, and personal prompts are not used to train public foundational models without your explicit consent.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="arrow-right-arrow-left" title="What happened to Nova Suite?">
    Nova Suite has officially been integrated into the EcoWestern ecosystem. As of March 1st, 2026, all legacy features have been upgraded and folded into our core platforms.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
