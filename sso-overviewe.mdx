## What is Economic SSO?

**Economic SSO** is the feature that lets you sign in once with your Economic ID and stay signed in as you move between different EcoWestern apps — without being asked to log in again.

Think of it like a theme park wristband: you show it at the gate once, and every ride recognizes it for the rest of the day.

---

## What does this look like in practice?

You're browsing an EcoWestern app when you decide to switch to another. Instead of seeing a login page, you land directly on your dashboard — already signed in. Your Economic ID session travels with you across EcoWestern automatically.

---

## Why do I sometimes get sent to the Economic ID sign-in page?

There are a few common reasons:

- **You haven't signed in yet** on this device or browser.
- **Your session expired** after an extended period of inactivity.
- **You signed out** (or changed your password), which ends your session everywhere.
- **You're using a different browser or a private/incognito window** — sessions are stored per browser, so a fresh window starts fresh.

In all cases, just sign in normally and you'll be sent straight back to where you were trying to go.

---

## Signing out across all apps

When you sign out of your Economic ID — from Economic ID Home or from any EcoWestern app — you are signed out of **every** EcoWestern app on that device at once.

This is the expected behavior and works this way by design, so you never get stuck partially signed in somewhere.

---

## What if I want to sign out of a device I no longer have?

If you've signed in on a device you've lost access to (like an old phone or a shared computer), change your password from Economic ID Home. This immediately ends all active sessions everywhere, across every device.

---

## Is my account information shared between apps?

EcoWestern apps know your name and email address so they can personalize your experience, but they **never** have access to your password or security settings — those stay with Economic ID only.
