# Installing eChat on Your Device

eChat is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**, which means it can be installed directly from your browser onto any device, no app store needed. Once installed, it behaves just like a native app: it launches from your home screen, runs in its own window, and works even with a spotty connection.

## Why install eChat?

| Feature | Browser tab | Installed PWA |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Home screen shortcut | ❌ | ✅ |
| Standalone app window | ❌ | ✅ |
| Push notifications | Limited | ✅ Full support |
| Faster load times | ✓ | ✅ Cached assets |
| Works offline / poor network | Limited | ✅ |

## Installing on Android

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open eChat in Chrome">
    Launch Google Chrome and navigate to the eChat app URL.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the install prompt">
    Chrome will display a banner at the bottom of the screen that says **"Add eChat to Home screen"**. Tap **Install**.

    If you don't see the banner, tap the **three-dot menu** (⋮) in the top-right corner and select **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm installation">
    A dialog will ask you to confirm. Tap **Install** or **Add**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch from your home screen">
    eChat now appears on your home screen like any other app. Tap it to open.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Installing on iPhone / iPad (iOS)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open eChat in Safari">
    iOS requires Safari to install PWAs. Open Safari and navigate to the eChat app URL.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the Share button">
    Tap the **Share** icon (the box with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select 'Add to Home Screen'">
    Scroll through the share sheet and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name and confirm">
    You can rename the shortcut if you like, then tap **Add** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open from your home screen">
    eChat will now appear on your iOS home screen. Tap it to launch.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Installing on Desktop (Windows, Mac, Linux)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open eChat in Chrome or Edge">
    Navigate to the eChat app URL in Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the install icon">
    In Chrome, look for a **computer with a download arrow** icon (⊕) in the address bar on the right. In Edge, look for a similar icon. Click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Install'">
    A prompt will appear. Click **Install** to confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch from your taskbar or app launcher">
    eChat is now installed as a desktop app. Find it in your Start menu, Applications folder, or directly on your taskbar/dock.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The in-app install banner

When you're using eChat in a browser, you may see an **install banner** at the top of the screen. This is eChat's built-in prompt encouraging you to install for the best experience:

- Tap **Install** to go straight to the installation steps for your device.
- Tap the **share icon** on the banner to copy your personal invite link and share eChat with a friend.
- Tap **✕** to dismiss the banner. It won't reappear during that session.

## Updating eChat

eChat updates automatically in the background. When a new version is ready, you'll see a small **update notification** at the bottom of the screen. Tap **Reload** to apply the update immediately, or the app will update the next time you launch it.
