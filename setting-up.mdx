# Setting Up Your Profile

When you sign in to eChat for the first time, you'll go through a short onboarding flow to set up your profile. Your profile is how other users find and recognize you.

## Onboarding walkthrough

After signing in, eChat automatically launches the onboarding screens:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your name">
    Type in your **first name**. This is used in conversation lists and call screens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a display name">
    Your **display name** is your public-facing handle, the name other users see in chats and when searching for you. Pick something you'd like to be known by.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload a profile photo">
    Tap the avatar circle to select a photo from your device. A clear, well-lit photo helps friends recognize you at a glance. You can skip this step and add a photo later in Settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your Chattercode">
    eChat automatically assigns you a unique **7-digit Chattercode**. This is your identifier on the platform, share it with friends so they can add you as a contact even if they don't know your email address.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What is a Chattercode?

Your Chattercode is a 7-digit number unique to your account. Think of it like a username that's guaranteed to be unique. You can find your Chattercode in your profile settings.

To share it:

- Screenshot your profile page and send it to someone.
- Read it out loud or copy-paste it into another message.

Anyone with your Chattercode can add you as a contact on eChat.

## Updating your profile later

You can update your profile at any time from the **Settings** page:

1. Open the eChat menu or navigate to **Settings**.
2. Tap your profile photo or name to edit them.
3. Make your changes and save.

<Info>
  Profile photo changes and display name updates are reflected in real time across all your conversations.
</Info>

## Profile visibility

Your display name and profile photo are visible to:

- Anyone who has added you as a contact.
- Anyone in a shared group or channel.

Your Chattercode is only shared when you choose to share it. Your email address is never displayed publicly.

<Tip>
  Choose a display name that's easy to spell and recognize. Friends will see this name when they search for you by Chattercode.
</Tip>
