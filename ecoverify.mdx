## Why account security matters

Your Economic ID is the key to every EcoWestern app you use. A compromised Economic ID means an attacker could access all of those services at once. Taking a few minutes to strengthen your account security is well worth it.

---

## EcoVerify

Two-factor authentication adds a second layer of verification at sign-in. Even if someone obtains your password, they cannot access your account without also having your second factor.

### How EcoVerify works in Economic ID

When EcoVerify is enabled, after entering your email and password you will be prompted to enter a **6-digit security code** generated by an authenticator app on your phone. The code refreshes every 30 seconds, so even if someone intercepts it, it's quickly useless.

### Setting up EcoVerify

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install an authenticator app">
    Download an authenticator app if you don't have one. Popular options include:
    - **Google Authenticator** (iOS / Android)
    - **Authy** (iOS / Android / desktop)
    - **1Password** (iOS / Android / desktop)
    - **Microsoft Authenticator** (iOS / Android)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open EcoVerify settings">
    Sign in to Economic ID and navigate to [id.ecowestern.net/id/home](https://id.ecowestern.net/id/home). Find the **Security** or **EcoVerify** section and click **Enable EcoVerify**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan the QR code">
    A QR code will be displayed. Open your authenticator app and scan it. The app will start generating 6-digit codes for your Economic ID account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a verification code">
    Enter the 6-digit code currently shown in your authenticator app to confirm that setup was successful.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your backup codes">
    You will be shown a set of **one-time backup codes**. Save these somewhere safe (for example, in your password manager). If you ever lose access to your authenticator app, a backup code lets you sign in and disable EcoVerify.

    <Warning>
      Each backup code can only be used **once**. Store them securely and do not share them.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Disabling EcoVerify

1. Sign in and navigate to **Economic ID Home → Security**.
2. Click **Disable EcoVerify** and confirm with your current password.
3. EcoVerify is removed immediately.

<Warning>
  Disabling EcoVerify reduces the security of your account. Only do this if you are switching authenticator apps or have a specific reason.
</Warning>

## Security best practices

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Use a strong, unique password" icon="lock">
    Choose a password you don't use anywhere else. A password manager can generate and store one for you.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Enable EcoVerify" icon="mobile">
    Turn on EcoVerify to protect your account even if your password is exposed in a data breach.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sign out on shared devices" icon="right-from-bracket">
    Always sign out of your Economic ID when using a shared or public computer.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Watch for phishing" icon="envelope-open-text">
    EcoWestern will never ask for your password over email or chat. Only enter your credentials on `id.ecowestern.net`.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Suspicious activity

If you notice unexpected sign-ins or believe your account has been compromised:

1. **Change your password immediately.** This revokes all active sessions everywhere.
2. **Enable EcoVerify** if it is not already on.
3. **Review connected apps** in Economic ID Home to check for any authorizations you do not recognize.
4. Contact EcoWestern support if you need further assistance.
