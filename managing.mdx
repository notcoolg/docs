# Managing Contacts

Your contacts are the people you can chat with on eChat. You can add anyone on the platform using their **Chattercode** or **email address**, even if you don't have them saved in your phone's contacts.

## How contacts work

eChat keeps your contact list separate from your phone's native address book. Your eChat contacts are people you've explicitly added — there's no automatic syncing with your phone contacts, which gives you full control over your privacy.

## Finding your own Chattercode

Your Chattercode is a unique 7-digit number assigned to your account. It's the easiest way for others to find and add you on eChat.

To find it:
1. Go to the **Home** screen or **Settings**.
2. Your Chattercode is displayed on your profile section.

Share your Chattercode with friends so they can add you.

## Adding a contact by Chattercode

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Home screen">
    Tap **Home** to see your contacts and the option to add someone new.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap 'Add Contact'">
    Tap the **Add Contact** button (the person+ icon or similar).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter their Chattercode">
    Type the 7-digit Chattercode of the person you want to add.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Tap **Add** or **Send Request**. The contact will be added to your list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adding a contact by email

If you know someone's email address:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Home screen">
    Tap **Home** to access the contact management area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap 'Add Contact'">
    Tap the **Add Contact** option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch to email search">
    If prompted, toggle to the **email** input method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter their email">
    Type the person's email address registered with eChat.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Tap **Add**. eChat will look up the account and add them to your contacts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You can only add someone by email if they have a registered eChat account with that email address.
</Note>

## Your contacts list

The **Home** screen shows your contacts list. From here you can:
- **See all your contacts** in one place
- **Tap a contact** to open a direct message conversation with them
- **Search your contacts** by name

## Starting a conversation with a contact

From the contacts list:
1. Tap a contact's name or avatar.
2. This opens (or creates) a DM conversation with that person.
3. Start typing to send your first message.

## Blocking a contact

If you need to stop receiving messages from someone:
1. Open a conversation with them.
2. Tap their name or the **info icon** at the top.
3. In the **Conversation Info** panel, select **Block User**.

Blocked users can no longer send you messages or call you.

## Viewing contact profiles

Tap a contact's name or avatar in any conversation to view their profile:
- Display name
- Profile photo
- Verified/official badge (if applicable)

## Verified accounts

Some accounts on eChat carry a **verified badge** indicating they are official or notable accounts. This badge helps you confirm you're messaging the right person or organization.
