## Accessing your account dashboard

After signing in, go to your **Economic ID Home** at [id.ecowestern.net/id/home](https://id.ecowestern.net/id/home). This is the central hub for all account settings.

---

## Changing your email address

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Economic ID Home">
    Navigate to [id.ecowestern.net/id/home](https://id.ecowestern.net/id/home).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Edit' next to your email">
    Find your current email address and click the edit icon or **Change Email** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your new email address">
    Type the new address and confirm it. Economic ID will send a verification link to the new address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the new address">
    Open the verification email sent to your new address and click the confirmation link.

    <Warning>
      Until you verify the new address your account continues to use the old one. The change is not applied until verification is complete.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Changing your password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Economic ID Home">
    Navigate to [id.ecowestern.net/id/home](https://id.ecowestern.net/id/home).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Change Password'">
    Locate the password section and click **Change Password**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter and confirm your new password">
    Choose a new password of at least 8 characters. Re-enter it to confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. All active sessions across all EcoWestern apps are immediately revoked for security — you will need to sign in again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use a unique, randomly generated password managed by a password manager. Avoid reusing passwords across services.
</Tip>

---

## Forgot your password?

If you cannot sign in because you forgot your password:

1. Go to [id.ecowestern.net/auth/login](https://id.ecowestern.net/auth/login).
2. Click **Forgot password?** below the sign-in form.
3. Enter your registered email address and click **Send Reset Link**.
4. Check your email for the reset link and follow the instructions.

<Note>
  Password reset links expire after **1 hour**. If yours expires, simply request a new one.
</Note>

---

## Signing out

To sign out of your Economic ID:

- From **Economic ID Home**, click the **Sign Out** button.
- Signing out logs you out of all EcoWestern apps on your current device at the same time.

To sign out on a device you no longer have access to, change your password. This revokes all sessions everywhere.

---

## Deleting your account

<Warning>
  Account deletion is **permanent**. All your data associated with EcoWestern services will be removed and cannot be recovered.
</Warning>

If you wish to delete your Economic ID:

1. Navigate to **Economic ID Home → Settings**.
2. Scroll to the **Danger Zone** section.
3. Click **Delete Account** and follow the confirmation steps.

If you experience any issues with account deletion, contact EcoWestern support.
