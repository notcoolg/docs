# Privacy & Security

eChat is built with privacy in mind. Here's an overview of the current privacy controls available and what's coming soon.

## Current privacy features

### Blocking users

If someone is bothering you or you no longer want to receive messages from them, you can block them:

1. Open a conversation with the person.
2. Tap their **name** or the **info icon** at the top.
3. In the **Conversation Info** panel, tap **Block User**.
4. Confirm the action.

Once blocked:

- They cannot send you messages.
- They cannot call you.
- You will not receive any notifications from them.

To unblock someone, go to their profile or the blocked users section in your settings.

### Leaving conversations

You can leave any group chat or channel at any time:

1. Open the **Conversation Info** panel.
2. Tap **Leave Conversation** or **Leave Group / Channel**.

After leaving, you won't receive any future messages from that conversation.

### Your Chattercode & discoverability

Your **Chattercode** is not public, you control who you share it with. No one can find you on eChat without knowing your Chattercode or email address.

Your **email address** is never shown to other users.

### Profile visibility

Your **display name** and **profile photo** are visible to:

- Anyone who has added you as a contact.
- Anyone in a shared group or channel.

They are **not** visible to users who haven't connected with you.

## Coming soon: End-to-end encryption

eChat has **end-to-end encryption (E2EE)** on its roadmap. When implemented, messages will be encrypted on your device before being sent, and only the intended recipient(s) will be able to decrypt and read them — not even eChat's servers will have access to the content.

Additional planned privacy features include:

- **Disappearing messages** — Set messages to automatically delete after a set time.
- **Read receipt controls** — Choose whether others can see when you've read their messages.
- **Online status controls** — Hide your "last seen" or online status from contacts.
- **Locked chats** — Protect specific conversations with a PIN or biometric authentication.
- **Device management** — View and revoke access to all devices signed into your account.

<Info>
  These features are in active development. Check back here for updates as they roll out.
</Info>

## Tips for staying safe on eChat

- **Only share your Chattercode** with people you trust or want to connect with.
- **Use a strong, unique password** for your eChat account.
- **Enable push notifications** so you can respond quickly to suspicious login attempts or messages.
- **Block and report** any user who sends you unsolicited or harmful messages.
- **Log out** of eChat on shared or public devices after use.

<Tip>
  If you ever receive a suspicious message or experience abuse on eChat, use the **Block** feature immediately and reach out to eChat support.
