# Sending GIFs

A great GIF is worth a thousand words. eChat has a built-in **GIF search** powered by NovaGIFs, so you can find and send the perfect animated clip in seconds.

## How to send a GIF

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a conversation">
    Navigate to any DM, group chat, or channel where you want to send a GIF.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the GIF icon">
    In the message input bar, tap the **GIF** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for a GIF">
    Type a keyword in the search field — for example, _"happy"_, _"thumbs up"_, _"excited"_, _"reaction"_ — and the results will update in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select and send">
    Tap the GIF you want to send. It's sent immediately to the conversation — no extra "Send" button needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Browsing trending GIFs

If you open the GIF picker without searching, you'll see a selection of **trending and popular GIFs** to inspire you. Browse through them and tap any one to send it.

## How GIFs appear in chat

GIFs appear as animated images inline in the conversation. They loop automatically and are displayed at a readable size so they don't take over the screen.

Tap a GIF to view it **full screen** in the media viewer. Tap **✕** to close.

## Tips for using GIFs

- **Keep it relevant** — GIFs add humor and personality, but one well-chosen GIF is better than several at once.
- **Search specifically** — The more specific your search term, the better the results. Try _"mic drop"_ instead of just _"drop"_.
- **Works in groups too** — GIFs are supported in group chats and channels, not just DMs.

---

<Tip>
  Search by emotion or situation rather than objects — terms like _"mind blown"_, _"awkward silence"_, or _"slow clap"_ tend to return the most expressive results.
</Tip>
