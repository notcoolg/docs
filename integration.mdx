## One login, every app

Your Economic ID is your single key to the entire EcoWestern ecosystem. Any EcoWestern app that supports Economic ID will recognize your session automatically — you sign in once and move freely between them.

---

## Supported apps

All EcoWestern products are built to work with Economic ID. When you visit an EcoWestern app for the first time (or on a new device), you'll be sent to the Economic ID sign-in page. After that, your login carries across automatically.

---

## What to expect when switching between apps

1. **Open any EcoWestern app.** If you are already signed in to your Economic ID, you land directly on the app's home screen — no login page.
2. **If you see a sign-in prompt**, just sign in with your Economic ID. You'll be sent right back to the app you were trying to reach.
3. **Your preferences and data** in each app are tied to your Economic ID, so they're always there regardless of which device you use.

## Getting the best experience

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Stay in the same browser" icon="browser">
    Your signed-in session lives in your browser. For the smoothest experience across multiple EcoWestern apps, use the same browser throughout your session.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Avoid private/incognito mode" icon="eye-slash">
    Private browsing windows start with no session, so you'll be asked to sign in each time. Use your regular browser window for the full Economic SSO experience.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Sign out when done on shared devices" icon="right-from-bracket">
    Because your session works across all apps, signing out in one place signs you out everywhere on that device. Always do this on shared or public computers.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Keep your details up to date" icon="user-check">
    Your name and email from Economic ID are what EcoWestern apps use to identify you. Keep them current in [Economic ID Home](https://id.ecowestern.net/id/home).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="An app keeps asking me to sign in even though I just did">
    Try the following:

    1. Make sure you completed the sign-in, check that you received and entered any EcoVerify code if prompted.
    2. Try signing out of Economic ID completely, then signing back in.
    3. Clear your browser's cookies and cache, then sign in again at [id.ecowestern.net/auth/login](https://id.ecowestern.net/auth/login).
    4. If the issue only happens in one specific app, try accessing that app from a different browser.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I signed in but was sent to the wrong place">
    After signing in, Economic ID returns you to the app that originally redirected you. If you ended up somewhere unexpected, navigate to the app directly — your session will still be active.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I'm signed in on my phone but not on my computer">
    Sessions are stored per browser and per device. Signing in on your phone doesn't automatically sign you in on your computer. Each device needs its own sign-in.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="An app isn't showing my correct name or email">
    Your display information comes from your Economic ID account. Update it in [Economic ID Home](https://id.ecowestern.net/id/home), then sign out and sign back in to the app so it picks up the latest details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
