# What Is eChat?

eChat is a modern, privacy-focused messaging and communication platform that works on any device with a web browser. It's built as a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**, which means you can install it directly from your browser, no app store required, and it works seamlessly on phones, tablets, and desktops.

eChat combines everything you'd expect from a full-featured messenger:

## Key highlights

- **Real-time messaging** — Messages are delivered instantly using live database subscriptions.
- **Voice & video calling** — Peer-to-peer calls with echo cancellation and noise suppression.
- **Rich media support** — Share photos, videos, GIFs (powered by Tenor), and record voice notes with waveform playback.
- **Message reactions** — React to messages with ❤️ 👍 👎 😂 😮 😢.
- **Read receipts** — Know when your messages have been seen.
- **Typing indicators** — See when someone is composing a reply.
- **Dark & light mode** — Designed to look great either way.
- **Install anywhere** — Works as a PWA on iOS, Android, and desktop.
- **Privacy-first roadmap** — End-to-end encryption coming soon.

## Who is eChat for?

eChat is for anyone who wants a fast, feature-rich messaging experience without being locked into a single platform or ecosystem. Whether you're catching up with friends, coordinating a team, or running a community channel, eChat adapts to how you communicate.
