## The simple version

Economic ID is one account that works everywhere in the EcoWestern ecosystem. Instead of creating a separate login for each EcoWestern app, you sign in once and you're good to go, across every app, automatically.

---

## What happens when you sign in

<Steps>
  <Step title="You open an EcoWestern app">
    You visit any EcoWestern app or service. If you're not already signed in, the app sends you to the Economic ID sign-in page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You sign in with your Economic ID">
    Enter your email and password (plus a quick code from your authenticator app if you have EcoVerify turned on).
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're signed in — everywhere">
    Once you sign in, your session is recognized across all EcoWestern apps in your browser. Open any other EcoWestern app and you'll already be signed in — no need to enter your password again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're sent back to where you started">
    After signing in, Economic ID automatically returns you to the app you were trying to reach.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## How long does my session last?

Your session stays active as long as you are using EcoWestern apps. If you step away for an extended period, you may be asked to sign in again for security. Signing out manually ends your session immediately across all EcoWestern apps on that device.

---

## What happens when I sign out?

When you sign out from any EcoWestern app or from Economic ID directly, you are signed out of **all** EcoWestern apps on that device at the same time — because they all share the same session.

---

## Your privacy

- Your password is never visible to — or stored by — any individual EcoWestern app. Only Economic ID handles your credentials.
- EcoWestern apps know who you are (your name and email), but never have access to your password or security details.
- Your account information is never sold or shared outside EcoWestern.
