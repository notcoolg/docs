# Managing Groups

Once a group is created, you can manage its members, settings, and metadata at any time, as long as you have the right permissions.

## Opening group info

To access group settings:

1. Open the group conversation.
2. Tap the **group name** or the **info icon** (ℹ) at the top of the conversation.
3. The **Conversation Info** panel will slide out from the side (on desktop) or appear as a screen (on mobile).

## Viewing members

The info panel lists all current group members, including their:

- Display name and avatar
- Role: **Owner**, **Admin**, or **Member**

## Adding members

To add someone to the group:

1. Open the **Conversation Info** panel.
2. Tap **Add Members** (or the \+ icon next to the members section).
3. Search for a contact and select them.
4. Tap **Add** to confirm.

New members are added as **Members** by default.

<Note>
  Only **Admins** and the **Owner** can add new members to a group.
</Note>

## Removing members

1. Open the **Conversation Info** panel.
2. Find the member you want to remove.
3. Tap their name or the options icon next to them.
4. Select **Remove from group**.

<Note>
  Only the **Owner** or **Admins** can remove members. You cannot remove the owner.
</Note>

## Member roles

| Role | Permissions |
| --- | --- |
| **Owner** | Full control, edit group, add/remove members, assign admins, delete group |
| **Admin** | Add/remove members, edit group name |
| **Member** | Send messages, view members |

### Promoting a member to Admin

1. Tap the member's name in the info panel.
2. Select **Make Admin** or the promote option.

### Demoting an Admin

1. Tap the admin's name in the info panel.
2. Select **Remove Admin** to revert them to Member status.

## Renaming the group

1. Open the **Conversation Info** panel.
2. Tap the **group name** at the top.
3. Type a new name and confirm.

<Note>
  Only **Admins** and the **Owner** can rename the group.
</Note>

## Leaving a group

If you no longer want to be part of a group:

1. Open the **Conversation Info** panel.
2. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Leave Group**.
3. Confirm your choice.

Once you leave, you'll no longer receive messages from that group. You can be re-added by any admin if needed.

<Warning>
  If you're the **Owner** and you leave, the group will continue but ownership responsibilities will need to be transferred to another member before you leave. You may be prompted to assign a new owner.
</Warning>

## Deleting a group

Owners can permanently delete a group:

1. Open the **Conversation Info** panel.
2. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Delete Group**.
3. Confirm the deletion.

This removes the group and all its messages for everyone. This action cannot be undone.

## Copying the group ID

For support or reference purposes, you can copy the group's unique ID from the **Conversation Info** panel. This is useful if you need to reference the conversation with eChat support.
