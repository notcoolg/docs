# Notifications

Stay on top of messages and incoming calls with eChat's notification system. eChat supports **push notifications** that arrive even when the app is closed or running in the background.

## Types of notifications

| Notification | When it triggers |
| --- | --- |
| **New message** | When you receive a message in any conversation |
| **Incoming call** | When someone starts a voice or video call with you |
| **Missed call** | When you miss an incoming call |

## Enabling push notifications

Push notifications require your browser's permission. Here's how to enable them:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Go to the **Settings** screen in eChat.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Notifications">
    Scroll to the **Notifications** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap 'Enable Notifications'">
    Tap the enable toggle or button. Your browser will display a permission prompt.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Allow notifications">
    In the browser prompt, tap or click **Allow**. eChat will register this device for push notifications.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Push notifications work best when eChat is [installed as a PWA](/installing) on your device. Installed apps receive notifications even when the browser is fully closed.
</Note>

## Disabling push notifications

To turn off notifications for eChat:

**Via eChat Settings:**

1. Go to **Settings → Notifications**.
2. Toggle push notifications off.

**Via your browser:**

1. Tap the **lock icon** (🔒) or **info icon** (ℹ) in the address bar.
2. Find **Notifications** and set it to **Block**.

## Notification sounds

eChat plays a sound when you receive a new message (if the app is open and active). You can turn this off:

1. Go to **Settings**.
2. Find the **Notification Sound** toggle.
3. Toggle it **off** to silence in-app message sounds.

<Note>
  Push notification sounds (when the app is in the background) are controlled by your device's system notification settings, not eChat's in-app settings.
</Note>

## Managing devices

eChat supports push notifications on up to **5 devices** simultaneously. This means you can receive notifications on your phone, tablet, and desktop at the same time.

To manage your registered devices:

- Go to **Settings → Notifications**.
- A list of registered notification devices is shown.
- Remove a device from the list if you no longer want it to receive notifications.

<Note>
  If you've reached the 5-device limit, you'll need to remove an existing device before adding a new one.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting notifications

| Issue | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| Not receiving notifications | Make sure you've allowed notifications in your browser settings |
| Notifications stopped working after reinstall | Re-enable notifications in eChat Settings to re-register this device |
| Notifications delayed | This can happen if your device is in battery saver mode — check OS notification settings |
| Getting too many notifications | Go to Settings and toggle off notification sounds, or disable push notifications for less active devices |
| No sound for notifications | Check your device's volume and that it's not on silent/Do Not Disturb |

---

<Tip>
  For the most reliable call notifications, install eChat as a PWA on your primary device and make sure push notifications are enabled. This ensures you never miss an incoming call.
</Tip>
