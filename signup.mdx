## Before you begin

- You need a valid **email address** that you can access.
- Passwords must be at least **8 characters** long.
- One Economic ID is enough for all EcoWestern services — you do not need to create a separate account per app.

---

## Creating your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the sign-up page">
    Navigate to [id.ecowestern.net/auth/signup](https://id.ecowestern.net/auth/signup) or click **Sign Up** in any EcoWestern app when prompted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email and choose a password">
    Fill in your email address and choose a strong password. Your password is stored securely and never visible to anyone at EcoWestern.

    <Tip>
      Use a unique password you don't use anywhere else, or generate one with a password manager.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Click **Create Account**. Economic ID will send a confirmation email to the address you provided.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Open the confirmation email from EcoWestern and click the **Verify Email** link. This confirms you own the address and activates your account.

    <Note>
      The verification link expires after **24 hours**. If it expires, return to the sign-up page and request a new one.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Once verified, you are redirected to the Economic ID login page. Enter your credentials and you will be taken to your **Economic ID Home** dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Already have an account?

Go straight to [id.ecowestern.net/auth/login](https://id.ecowestern.net/auth/login) to sign in.

## Troubleshooting sign-up

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I didn't receive the verification email">
    1. Check your **spam / junk** folder.
    2. Make sure you entered your email address correctly.
    3. Wait a few minutes — delivery can occasionally be delayed.
    4. If the email still hasn't arrived after 10 minutes, return to the sign-up page and re-enter your details to trigger a new verification email.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The verification link says it has expired">
    Verification links are valid for **24 hours**. If yours has expired, go back to the sign-up page and re-submit your email to receive a fresh link.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I get an error saying the email is already in use">
    An account with that email address already exists. Try signing in at [id.ecowestern.net/auth/login](https://id.ecowestern.net/auth/login). If you have forgotten your password, use the **Forgot password** link on the login page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The page shows a generic error">
    Try refreshing the page and submitting again. If the problem persists, try opening the page in a private/incognito window, or contact EcoWestern support.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
