## Welcome to Economic ID

**Economic ID (Economic ID)** is EcoWestern's central identity and authentication platform. It provides a single, secure account that grants access to every app and service in the EcoWestern ecosystem — no more creating a separate username and password for each product.

Think of Economic ID as your universal passport for EcoWestern: sign in once and move freely between all connected applications.

## Key terms

| Term | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Economic ID** | Your EcoWestern account — one login that works across all connected services. |
| **Economic ID Portal** | The sign-in and account management page at `id.ecowestern.net`. |
| **Economic SSO** | The feature that keeps you signed in as you move between EcoWestern apps — no repeated logins. |
